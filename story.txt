Ricky Bobby never imagined that his quiet life in a small town would involve a high-speed chase in a stolen Porsche, but there he was, shaking and baking. As Ricky hurriedly glanced into his rearview mirror, seeing the Raesfeld police lights close behind him, all he could muster into a thought was 'I am not getting arrested wearing Dirndl'. Ricky picked up the bratwurst from his lederhose and wished he would've stopped by the Biergarten, before stepping on the gas.
